Complete Restaurant Solution

QR Ordering

Scan → browse menu with modifiers → pay → ticket routed to KDS/print. Supports table numbers and takeaway.

POS System

Fast order entry, discounts, voids, and receipts. Works with compatible printers and payment terminals.
